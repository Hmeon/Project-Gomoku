# Requirements Summary

- Follow Gomoku-Pro openings: black at center, white anywhere, black third move outside 5x5 center.
- Exact-five wins; 6+ does not win. No additional forbidden moves.
- Board size: 15x15 or 19x19; cells: -1 black, 0 empty, 1 white.
- Move limit: 5 seconds per turn; invalid or late moves disqualify.
- Core files: main.py, Omokgame.py, Board.py, Stone.py, Player.py, AI files named by student ID/initials.
- League play: teams play both colors; ties resolved by extra games or rock-paper-scissors per PDF.
